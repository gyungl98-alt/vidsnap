About VidSnap OCR

VidSnap is an AI-enhanced OCR tool built for fast extraction of text from images and videos. It includes language auto-detection, table support, smart paragraph formatting, and export options like TXT/PDF/DOCX/XLSX/RTF.

यह सेवा आपको सरल, तेज़ और सुरक्षित OCR ऑपरेशन देती है।

Developed as an open-source/learning project to help creators, students, and businesses automate document extraction workflows.

Supported Document Types & OCR Tips

VidSnap can effectively extract text from various types of documents and media. Here's a comprehensive guide to get the best results:

📸 Images - Best for Static Documents

Supported Formats: JPEG, PNG, BMP, TIFF, GIF, WebP

Perfect for: Scanned documents, photos of text, screenshots, receipts, invoices, ID cards, business cards, handwritten notes, forms, certificates

Pro Tips for Best OCR Results:

  • 📏 Resolution: Use images at least 300 DPI (dots per inch) for crisp text
  • 💡 Lighting: Ensure even, bright lighting without harsh shadows
  • 📐 Alignment: Keep text straight and properly oriented (not tilted)
  • 🎨 Contrast: High contrast between text and background
  • 🚫 Avoid: Blurry images, curved pages, wrinkled paper, watermarks, or busy backgrounds
  • 📱 Mobile Photos: Hold camera steady, use flash in low light, avoid zoom

🎥 Videos - Best for Dynamic Content

Supported Formats: MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV, WMV, FLV, WebM, 3GP

Perfect for: Lecture recordings, presentations, tutorials, documentaries, news clips, educational videos, training materials, subtitles, on-screen text

Pro Tips for Best OCR Results:

  • 🎬 Video Quality: HD resolution (1080p or higher) for better text clarity
  • 📝 Text Size: Ensure text is large and readable on screen
  • 🎭 Text Style: Clear, sans-serif fonts work best (avoid decorative fonts)
  • ⚡ Playback Speed: Normal speed videos extract better than fast-forward
  • 📊 Frame Rate: 24-30 FPS videos provide optimal frame sampling
  • 🚫 Avoid: Fast-moving text, shaky camera, low resolution, compressed videos

📄 Scanned Documents - Best for Professional Content

Document Types: PDFs, Word documents, scanned papers, forms, contracts, reports, books, magazines

Perfect for: Business documents, legal papers, academic materials, technical manuals, newspapers, brochures

Pro Tips for Best OCR Results:

  • 🖨️ Scanner Settings: 300-600 DPI, grayscale or color mode
  • 📏 Paper Size: Standard A4/Letter size documents
  • 📐 Flat Scanning: No wrinkles, folds, or curved pages
  • 🧹 Clean Source: Dust-free, no stains or marks on paper
  • 📚 Book Scanning: Use book cradle or flatbed scanner for bound materials
  • 🚫 Avoid: Yellowed paper, faded ink, glossy surfaces, double-sided scans

💻 Digital Screenshots - Best for UI/UX Content

Screen Types: Desktop screenshots, mobile app screens, website captures, software interfaces

Perfect for: Error messages, UI text, code snippets, tutorials, documentation, chat logs

Pro Tips for Best OCR Results:

  • 🖥️ Resolution: Native screen resolution (avoid zoomed screenshots)
  • 🔍 Zoom Level: 100% zoom for accurate text capture
  • 🎨 Theme: Light backgrounds with dark text work best
  • 📱 Mobile Screenshots: Use built-in screenshot tools, avoid cropped images
  • 🚫 Avoid: Low-resolution images, angled screenshots, blurry captures

📝 Handwritten Notes - Limited Support

Note Types: Clean handwritten text, printed handwriting, signatures

Best Results: Clear, consistent handwriting with good spacing

Limitations: Cursive writing, messy handwriting, or stylized scripts may not extract well

🌐 Language Support

Primary Languages: English, Hindi, Arabic, Chinese (Simplified/Traditional), Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Odia

Auto-Detection: VidSnap automatically detects the primary language in your content

Mixed Languages: Documents with multiple languages are supported

⚡ Performance Tips

  • File Size: Keep files under 50MB for faster processing
  • Batch Processing: Process similar documents together for consistency
  • Output Formats: Choose format based on your needs (PDF for preservation, DOCX for editing, XLSX for data)
  • Quality Check: Always review OCR results, especially for critical documents

💡 Pro Tip: For best results, combine multiple techniques - use high-quality scans with good lighting, clear fonts, and proper alignment. If you're unsure about a document type, try uploading a small sample first!
