About VidSnap OCR

VidSnap is an AI-enhanced OCR tool built for fast extraction of text from images and videos. It includes language auto-detection, table support, smart paragraph formatting, and export options like TXT/PDF/DOCX/XLSX/RTF.

यह सेवा आपको सरल, तेज़ और सुरक्षित OCR ऑपरेशन देती है।

Developed as an open-source/learning project to help creators, students, and businesses automate document extraction workflows.
