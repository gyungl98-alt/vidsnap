About VidSnap OCR

VidSnap is an AI-enhanced OCR tool built for fast extraction of text from images and videos. It includes language auto-detection, table support, smart paragraph formatting, and export options like TXT/PDF/DOCX/XLSX/RTF.

यह सेवा आपको सरल, तेज़ और सुरक्षित OCR ऑपरेशन देती है।

Developed as an open-source/learning project to help creators, students, and businesses automate document extraction workflows.

Supported Document Types & OCR Tips

VidSnap can effectively extract text from various types of documents and media. Here's a preview of what works best:

📸 Images

  • Formats: JPEG, PNG, BMP, TIFF, GIF
  • Best for: Scanned documents, photos of text, screenshots, receipts, invoices, ID cards
  • Tips for better OCR:
    • Use high-resolution images (at least 300 DPI)
    • Ensure good lighting and contrast
    • Keep text straight and aligned
    • Avoid blurry or distorted images
    • Remove shadows or glare

🎥 Videos

  • Formats: MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV, WMV, FLV
  • Best for: Lecture recordings, presentations, documentaries, tutorials with on-screen text
  • Tips for better OCR:
    • Use videos with clear, readable text overlays
    • Ensure stable camera and good lighting
    • Avoid fast-moving text or shaky footage
    • Text should be large and high-contrast
    • Minimize background noise or distractions

📄 Documents

  • Types: PDFs, Word documents (converted to images), handwritten notes (limited)
  • Best for: Typed documents, forms, certificates, books
  • Tips for better OCR:
    • Use clean, typed text rather than handwriting
    • Ensure text is not too small or stylized
    • Scan documents at high resolution
    • Straighten pages before scanning
    • Avoid wrinkled or damaged paper

Note: VidSnap supports multiple languages including English, Hindi, and others. For best results, ensure your content matches these guidelines!
