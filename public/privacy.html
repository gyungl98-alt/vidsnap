Privacy Policy

This site collects no personal user data on its own. Uploaded files are processed in memory (or temporary server storage) and deleted after processing, per internal policy.

Data Handling

  • Files must be user-uploaded; no automatic scanning is performed.
  • Temporary upload artifacts are cleaned up timely.
  • No third-party tracking is embedded by default; if you place AdSense, standard Google data policies apply.

Cookies and Analytics

Currently no cookies are set by the app itself; basic usage logs may be collected by hosting provider.
